  • Vision & Mission

Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services (BASIS) is the national trade body for Software & IT Enabled Service industry of Bangladesh. Established in 1997, the association has been working with a vision of developing vibrant software & IT service industry in the country. Currently BASIS has over 700 members who contribute the majority in the Software & IT service industry in Bangladesh.

To address the skill gap of HR in the industry, BASIS started its own training activities in 2007. Later in 2012, BASIS institutionalized its training activities and set up BASIS Institute of Technology & Management (BITM) with the support of World Bank. BITM was established with a vision to be a world-class IT institute in Bangladesh for the purpose of enhancing the competitiveness of the IT Sector in Bangladesh by creating a pool of qualified IT professionals and quality certified IT companies.

To become a Center of Excellence in Information Technology and Knowledge Management for a more proactive and responsive extension service through innovation, dedicated research and development teams and management expertise tied with a strong technology focus.
